**Problem**: On the study page, the notes content is not properly centered on the screen due to the fixed sidebar layout.

**Location**: `/src/app/study/page.tsx`, lines 529-592 (Layout section) and lines 570-591 (Notes main area)

**Current Implementation**:
- Outer container uses `display: flex` with:
  - Fixed sidebar TOC (width: 220px, position: fixed)
  - In-flow spacer (width: 220px)

**Solution**: 
Modify the layout to center the notes content within the viewport while maintaining the sidebar functionality. We'll adjust the flex container to properly center the content.

**Changes**:

2. Remove the fixed widths and use a different approach for sidebar spacing
3. Ensure the notes area is properly centered
